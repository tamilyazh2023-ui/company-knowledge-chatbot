AI enhances security in Windows 11 through a variety of features designed to protect against evolving cyber threats and to simplify security for users and IT professionals. AI is used in the Smart App Control* in Windows 11 to predict which apps are safe to download and run while blocking unknown apps often associated with new malware. This proactive approach is designed to simplify security and make Windows more secure out of the box. *Only available on the latest version of Windows 11. In order to keep you as safe as possible, you must start with a fresh system. PCs that have been running previous versions of Windows must do a full reset of the Windows 11 operating system for the feature to work. Only available in North America and Europe. How can I stay up to date on the latest AI features in Windows? Windows 11 helps ensure your device remains secure and current by automatically downloading and installing updates including the latest AI features. Furthermore, users have the option to update the pre-installed apps to their latest versions via Microsoft Store , independent of the Windows 11 OS updates. Microsoft Store will automatically update apps in the background. If your PC is managed by an organization, it may be that they disable updates. Contact your administrator to find out how your device may be updated with the latest features.
